Welcome!

Mass Effect 5e is a D&D 5e conversion project for the Mass Effect universe. Start by reading the Rules to review the changes and tweaks. The rest of the site acts as your virtual Player's Handbook, with all the information on classes, races, weapons, etc. If you'd like to look at the underlying code, checkout the repo.
