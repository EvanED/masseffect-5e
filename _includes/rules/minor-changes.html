Minor Changes

Melee Attacks

The only real melee attacks are made with your Omni-tool. Each class has a unique melee attack.

Distance

I use meters instead of feet. Why? Because this is set in the future and it's sci-fi and using metric just seems to make more sense. The general rule of thumb is that 3ft = 1m.

Carry Capacity

Carrying Capacity is point based, not pound based. Your capacity is equal to Strength score. You can can carry up to 5 additional points of weapons at the expense of a 2m reduction in movement per point. If you are carrying the maximum 5 points over, all of your Dexterity checks are made at a disadvantage. You cannot carry more than 5 more points over your capacity.

Armor

Armor proficiency is based on race not class.

Instead of the different types of light, medium, and heavy armor, (studded, chain mail, etc.) there are only the 3 major types (light, medium, and heavy). Armor does take away from your carry capacity.

Type Armor Class (AC) Stealth Weight
Light 11 + Dex modifier - 1
Medium 14 + Dex modifier (max 2) - 2
Light 17 Disadvantage 3

There are plenty of armor bonuses in Mass Effect. I've not created a list (yet?), but you and your GM should be creative about armor bonuses.

Conditions

Lift: Targets dangle helplessly in the air. They are considered Restrained. While lifted, the target is primed for combos.

Frozen: Considered petrified, but only last for 1 round. The creature is not turned to stone but frozen solid. It has resistances to all damage (like petrification).
